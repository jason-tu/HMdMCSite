Procedural content generation using Markov models. Generating video game maps.

In this paper we explore Markov models as a means of modeling and generating content for multiple domains. We apply our Markov models to Super Mario Bros., Loderunner, and Kid Icarus in order to determine how well our models perform in terms of the playability of the content generated, the expressive ranges of the models, and the effects of training data on those expressive ranges.

Drexel 2016
