How to use

1) Choose the game of your preference;

2) Load your training map, or use the default ones.

3) Choose a method to generate maps. For while, we just have the MdMC method implemented. For doubts, information, or access to the C++ version of the code in Sam's Website;

4) Choose the height and width for the map that will be generated;

5) Set the configuration that best fit your needs in the Experimental Set-ups;

6) Click on Training Maps button;

7) Click on Sampling Maps button;

8) Click on Text Output, and Graphic Output tabs in order to see the generated maps.

P.S. if you are not generating a Super Mario, Loderunner, or Kid Icarus map it is not possible to see the Graphic Output.
