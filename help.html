How to use

1) Choose the game of your preference.

2) Load your training map, or use the default ones.

3) Choose a method to generate maps. As of now, we only have the MdMC method implemented. For doubts, information, or access to the C++ version of the code, see Sam's Website;

4) Choose the generated map's height and width.

5) Set the configuration that best fit your needs in Experimental Set-ups.

6) Click on Training Maps button;

7) See the Text Output and Graphic Output tabs to see the generated map(s).

Note: There will only be graphical output for Mario, Loderunner, or Kid Icarus.
Only Mario and Loderunner are "playable".
