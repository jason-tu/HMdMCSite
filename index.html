Procedural content generation using Markov models. Generating video game maps.

The purpose of this website is to provide a tool that allows people to use Markov models as a means of modeling and generating content for multiple domains. It is possible to apply Markov models to Super Mario Bros, Loderunner, Kid Icarus, or to your own game maps. This way, you can choose the configuration that best fit your needs and explore the possibilities of the usage of Markov models to procedural content generation.
